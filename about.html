S&S DUO

S&S Duo are an upcoming, vibrant and very talented Amapiano duo from Johannesburg. The duo is comprised of Speedy and SpheDaDeejay.

The name Speedy dates back to his early childhood days. Growing up in Meadowlands Zone 11 Soweto, Speedy loved anything that had to do with music. He started with a computer software called “Virtual DJ” where he learned how to compile and mix music. As time went by, he figured that he needs to learn how to actually produce his own music because he believes without music there’s no life. Speedy wanted to make his own music and get it out there for people to love and enjoy. After moving from Meadowlands to Fleurhof, he started buying producing equipment bit by bit to start doing what he loves. In that period in his life, that’s where he became friends with Sphe who also has the love for music.

SpheDaDeejay is a young upcoming Amapiano producer and DJ from Fluerhof. He fell in love with the Amapiano genre in 2019, which is the same year that he started producing Amapiano. Sphe grew up in Emndeni Soweto, where he started his career as a DJ by learning how to DJ using an app called “crossdj” later he was also introduced to “Virtual DJ” and from that he went to “Cdj’s”.

Speedy and SpheDaDeejay met in 2020 and started working on some music together and on a later stage decided to work as a duo which they named themselves S&S Duo. Through learning FL Studio from YouTube tutorials and basic skills, they started with their first project.
